Example Stack

Your VPS template is alive.

This sample app proves the routing pattern for future projects: keep the app private on Docker networks, give it a stable alias, and let Caddy handle the public entrypoint.

  • Public traffic terminates at Caddy on ports 80 and 443.
  • Internal services share the external Docker networks web and internal.
  • New projects can copy this folder and replace the image, volumes, and upstream domain.
